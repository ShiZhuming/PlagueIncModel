$$ \frac{dE}{dt} = \alpha SI_{eff} - \beta E $$
$$ \frac{dI}{dt} = \beta E - (\gamma 
+ \delta)I $$
$$ \frac{dR}{dt} = \gamma $$
$$ \frac{dD}{dt} = \delta I $$
